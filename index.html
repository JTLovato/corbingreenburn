I Capture The Beauty In Darkness.

Welcome to a portfolio where darkness is not an absence, but a presence that breathes life into every frame. I was raised in a smal town near Boise, where I found my passion for photography wandering through old neighborhoods and overgrown gardens, drawn to the interplay of shadow and light. My early fascination with the atmosphere of forgotten places and fleeting moments led me to develop a style that blends moody landscapes with intimate portraits, always searching for beauty in the overlooked and the in-between.

Now based in San Fransisco, I continue to explore themes of solitude, nostalgia, and transformation through my lens. My portfolio reflects a lifelong curiosity for the poetic side of everyday life, inviting viewers to pause and find meaning in the subtle drama of the world around us. My hope is that my photographs invite viewers to pause, reflect, and perhaps find a bit of beauty in the unexpected.

Through My Lens, The Shadows Tell A Story.

My specialties lie in atmospheric portraiture, moody landscapes, and evocative studies of architecture, all infused with a distinctively alternative or gothic sensibility. I love working with dramatic lighting and expressive poses, often seeking out locations like old churches, ruins, or misty woods to give my portraits a sense of narrative and emotional depth. I'm especially drawn to the interplay of light and shadow, using techniques like chiaroscuro and backlighting to create images that feel mysterious and quietly romantic.

Beyond portraits, I have a passion for capturing the haunting beauty of gothic architecture and natural scenes, using creative composition and post-processing to highlight texture, contrast, and mood. I enjoy incorporating props, costumes, and thoughtful styling to craft visually rich scenes that blend historical references with a modern edge. Whether I'm shooting on film or digitally, my goal is always to evoke intrigue, solitude, and the poetic side of everyday life through my photography.
